mark [dot] thomas @ dal [dot] ca

I am third-year PhD candidate in computer science @ Dalhousie University under the supervision of Stan Matwin. My main interests are in applying deep learning to the area of bioacoustics. Specifically, I work on algorithms to detect the vocalizations of endangered baleen whales within acoustic recordings.

I hold a MSc and BSc from Acadia University. During my MSc, I was supervised by Pritam Ranjan and Holger Teismann and worked on Bayesian optimization using Gaussian processes.
